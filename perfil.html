Sobre mim

Extensive Salesforce expertise with almost 6 years in roles ranging from Consultant and Architect to Technical Leader, with experience across multi-cloud projects including Service Cloud, Sales Cloud, Experience Cloud, B2C, Salesforce Maps, Field Service, and Communication Cloud.

✅ Hard Skills

  • Requirements Gathering: Collection and detailing of business requirements.
  • Designed Solutions: Creation of scalable and business-aligned solutions.
  • Project Documentation: Clear documentation of scope, requirements, and solutions.
  • API Refinement: Review and adjustment of APIs for efficient integrations.
  • Data Modeling: Data modeling aligned with Salesforce architecture.
  • Salesforce Configuration: Configuration of Salesforce clouds using native features.
  • CPQ, OM, Catalog, EPC: Experience with product modeling and commercial processes.
  • SF Native Features: Advanced use of flows, reports, dashboards, and automation tools.

✅ Soft Skills

  • Leadership: Coordination of technical teams with focus on best practices.
  • Problem Solving: Fast and effective resolution of complex problems.
  • Expectation Management: Clear alignment between technical teams and business areas.
  • Negotiation: Negotiation of scope, timelines, and priorities with stakeholders.
  • Collaboration: Collaborative work within cross-functional squads.
